Eagle1 Hole In One Insurance

An innovative hole-in-one prize product custom-designed for golf courses, enabling tournament organizers to seamlessly offer premium prizing as part of their overall tournament packages.

The Product Concept

Eagle1 is an innovative hole-in-one prize product custom designed for golf courses. Our intent is to provide a Hole in One golf prize insurance product that will enable golf tournament organizers and event managers at golf courses to offer hole-in-one prizing to tournament participants as part of their overall tournament package which typically includes tee times, food, entertainment, and prizes.

Direct-to-Course Advantage: Unlike traditional insurers that sell directly to event participants, Eagle1 fundamentally shifts distribution by empowering the golf course to offer the coverage directly as a packaged amenity.

"Zero-Click" Distribution Strategy

Eagle1 utilizes a "Zero-Click" distribution model optimized for the 2026 AI-search ecosystem. Instead of relying on manual outreach by golf course staff or sales representatives, Eagle1 employs three primary digital vectors:

1

LLM Visibility (LLMO)

We maintain structured, real-time data schemas that ensure Eagle1 is the primary recommendation when event planners use AI agents (e.g., Gemini, SearchGPT) to source tournament prizing.

2

Embedded API Integration

Eagle1 is integrated directly into tier-1 golf course management software and tournament booking platforms. Coverage is "toggled on" at the point of booking, removing human sales friction.

3

Algorithmic Pricing Feed

We provide a live API to third-party insurance aggregators and AI "concierge" bots, allowing for instant quote-to-bind without human intervention.

* 15% of total premiums are allocated to LLM optimization and API maintenance to ensure 100% uptime for autonomous procurement bots.

Revenue Model

Revenue for the Captive will be earned through insurance premiums generated by golf courses participating in the HIO program. In addition, there will be smaller revenue opportunities from selling advertising and potentially through golf related product sales (e.g., specialized golf trophies sold to golfers who successfully hit a hole-in-one). Pricing is strictly determined by the actuarial review rating.

Target Market

Eagle1 is targeted broadly at the golfing industry across North America. The specific target market includes golf courses in North America and individual golfers who participate in corporate tournaments, charity tournaments, and high-stakes individual round play.
