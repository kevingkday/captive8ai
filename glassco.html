GlassCo Insurance

A purpose-built captive insurance company providing highly profitable glass replacement insurance for residential and commercial premises in British Columbia.

The Product Concept

This is a specialized glass replacement and repair insurance product designed exclusively for residential and commercial premises. Coverage provides for the free replacement of exterior plain glass windows and glass panels in doors at the covered premises.

Coverage Scope: Applies solely with respect to accidental breakage of glass, or breakage caused by criminal vandalism.

Target Market

The primary target market consists of residential and commercial premises across the Province of British Columbia. This product is structured to be distributed efficiently through networks of licensed insurance brokerages.

Program History

This fundamental product model has been successfully sold in British Columbia for more than 10 years, originally as an unlicensed warranty-style product. Pending FSTOM requirements dictate that all glass repair and replacement contracts must transition into fully licensed insurance products, establishing the immediate strategic necessity for this captive formation.
